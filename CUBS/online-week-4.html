Scavenger hunt

When I say go, try and find these items:
The first person to find them all and put their hand up wins!

  • A kitchen utensil (e.g. a ladel)

  • Something that starts with A, B or C

  • Something that starts with X, Y or Z

  • A salt or pepper shaker

  • A musical insrument

  • Something that rhymes with spot

  • Something that rhymes with mat

Who am I?

In this game, one person leaves the room for 30 seconds.
While they are gone everyone agrees on what that person is, e.g. The person is the Prime Minister.
When they come back, everyone is muted and needs to mime to the person what they are.
The faster they guess who they are the better.
