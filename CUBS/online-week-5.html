ANZAC day - Virtual dawn service

This year, because of the COVID-19 virus the dawn service cannot be help in the usual way.
We can still however remember those who faught for us by participating in the virtual dawn service.

We can join in with the service at 6am on 25 April by standing at our letterboxes, front doors and other places while staying within our bubbles.
Then we can tune into the service on the RNZ NATIONAL radio station or the online live stream.

We will remember them
